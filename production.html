Production

In 2014, “Kakhetian Traditional Winemaking” has invested in construction of 2 new fruit processing facilities in Western Georgia. They were put into operation in September, 2015 undertheNENAtrademark.

NENA produces a wide range of fruitjams, compotes,nectarsand sauces, including original Georgian plum sauce Tkemali and authentic hot Adjika. We also produce several varieties of high-qualitynaturalhoney.

We are proud with the high quality of fruits and vegetables grown inGeorgia, andwe always use 100% natural ingredients, harvested and processed by the localfarmers. NENA products do not contain antibiotics, pesticides, GMO or other substances prohibited in GeorgiaandEU.
